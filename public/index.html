About

What’s this Battle of the Beards thing?

For learning

Great talks on a variety of IT topics during the day

For good

All funds raised over the costs of the event go to CALM, the Campaign Against Living Miserably

For fun

A nerd Bring-and-Buy, big screen gaming, pool, and happy hour in the evening

The plan

Here’s what’s happening

  • Now

    Open for talks

    Submit your talks today. Donate your time to educate people and help raise awareness of male suicide. Anyone bearded can present - no need for it be a real beard though! Enter the Battle of the Beards!

  • January 15, 2017

    Registration opens

    You’ll be able to register for the event from the 15th of January. It costs £15 to cover the costs for you during the day. You can pay as much as you like above £15, and all proceeds go to CALM

  • February 15-21, 2017

    Talks selected

    We will select talks and announce the agenda

  • March 30, 2017

    BATTLE OF THE BEARDS

    Battle of the Beards commences from 8:30am at the Bier Keller in Cardiff
