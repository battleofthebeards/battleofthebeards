About

Battle of the Beards is a tech conference making a difference.
Spend the day learning about different areas of IT, spend the evening having fun, and spend a little bit of cash to help a male suicide charity.

Beards are not a requirement to come along for the day!

29 March 2017

Battle of the Beards takes place on March 29th, doors opening at 8:30am

Beard-Keller

Battle of the Beards will be fought at the Bierkeller

Low cost

Tickets will be a minimum of £15, with pay-as-you-like above that going to charity

Informative

There’ll be nifty case studies, introductions to languages, general computing talks around security, ethics, design patterns etc, and so on

Doing good

Suicide disproportionately affects men. Any funds raised over the cost of the event will go to Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM), a male suicide awareness and prevention charity

Lots of fun

As well as great talks, we’re going to have some beardy fun. Nerd Bring-and-Buy, big screen gaming, pool, happy hour in the evening, and more

The plan

Here’s what’s happening

  • Now

    Open for talks

    Submit your talks today. Donate your time to educate people and help raise awareness of male suicide. Anyone bearded can present - no need for it be a real beard though! Enter the Battle of the Beards!

  • January 15, 2017

    Registration opens

    You’ll be able to register for the event from the 15th of January. It costs £15 to cover the costs for you during the day. You can pay as much as you like above £15, and all proceeds go to Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM)

  • February 15-21, 2017

    Talks selected

    We will select talks and announce the agenda

  • March 29, 2017

    BATTLE OF THE BEARDS

    Battle of the Beards commences from 8:30am at the Bierkeller
